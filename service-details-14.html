Government Legal Transactions & Notarisation

Our Govt Legal Transactions service manages notarisation, attestation, power of attorney preparation, and all government filing required for legal and commercial transactions.

We coordinate with ministries, courts and public authorities to ensure documents are correctly prepared, legalized and submitted — reducing delays and avoiding rejections.

  • Document notarisation & legalisation
  • Power of Attorney drafting & execution
  • Attestations for corporate & personal documents
  • Court filings & ministry submissions
  • Authentication and embassy/legal apostille support
  • Document translation & certified copies
  • Legal follow-up and retrieval services

Core Govt Legal Services

We ensure legal documents are correctly drafted, authenticated and submitted to the relevant authorities with minimal friction.
